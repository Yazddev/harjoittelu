üìä Understanding Variance Explanation

What does this mean?

  • 59% of job satisfaction differences can be explained by the factors studied
  • 40% of job turnover intentions are predictable from workplace conditions
  • 31-45% of career satisfaction variance is accounted for by structural and personal factors

This is remarkably high for social science research, indicating strong practical significance!

‚úÖ Success Drivers - Deep Dive

These factors are modifiable by management and directly impact retention!
